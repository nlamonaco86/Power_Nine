About Power_Nine

This project began as a learning exercise in DOM Manipulation using AJAX requests and Javascript/JQuery in conjuction with the Scryfall API. It features the ability to search for individual cards, which then appear as clickable thumbnails in the virtual binder below. The cards appear 9 at a time, just as they would in a physical collection. It also has the ability to search and display entire sets in the same way, along with brief information about it. Previously searched cards and sets can be recalled, undone or cleared.

Future Development

Future development plans include completely styling the page with an improved UI, in addition to improved and more efficient AJAX functionality. The binder will also be made to include no more than 9 cards at a time, and start a new page upon a 10th addition. Future releases are also planned to have integration with multiple API's to display other sports and non-sports collectible cards as well as current financial value. They will also feature the ability to store and save custom sets through social media or other platforms.

How do I use this?

Although the application currently only displays Magic: The Gathering cards, no knowledge of this particular game is required. Try it yourself, and search for classic cards such as Force of Will, Master Transmuter, or Riptide Director. For the set search function, try and view classics such as Alpha, Tempest, or Mirage. Just about anyone might find artwork or flavor text that catches their eye.
